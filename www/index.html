FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can enroll in SF MechRewards Program?

    SF MechRewards is a platform for automotive mechanics & engineers to come together and work towards the betterment of their community, to learn critical business skills and to grow professionally and socially.

  • How to Enroll in SF MechRewards Program?

    To enroll, first � get your login credentials from-

    • Local SF Batteries officer/retailer/garage/distributor
    • Nearest Battery shop
    • Download SF MechRewards App from Play Store
    • Use your login credentials to login & experience SF MechRewards!

  • How to Redeem my earned points?

    Go to "Redeem Points" option to redeem your points. SF MechRewards platform offers only PayTM money/wallet option to redeem earned points.

  • What are the other features & benefits of SF MechRewards program?

    Different USPs of the app are:-

    • Dashboard � All your account information including lifetime points scanned, redeemed and current balance; view tier status and SF Batteries contact details.
    • Scan � Bank all your SF MechRewards points using the in app QR/Barcode scanner and get them added in your personal SF MechRewards membership account. The scanner allows multiple code scanning
    • Redeem Gift � Redeem your earned points through PayTM
    • What's New Section � Browse our latest promotions, events, product launches and many more!
    • Videos � Stay updated with all the latest SF Batteries related product and training videos in one place. Featured video displays the latest, trending video with an option to share it with your friends and family
    • Product Catalogues � Explore the range of SF Batteries at your fingertips � From Automotive Batteries to Inverters to HUPS systems and More!

  • Any app permissions required for SF MechRewards program?

    Permissions Requested:

    • Camera - To enable scanning of SF Batteries QR & Barcodes labels
    • Location - To identify your location and provide relevant offers and information near you
    • Storage - To store photos captured by you for later access
